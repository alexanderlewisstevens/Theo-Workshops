## What to Post (High Signal, Low Noise)

Post cadence: 2 posts/month. Each post must include a concrete artifact.

- Post type 1: mini methods note (problem, data, model, one plot, one limitation).
- Post type 2: conference/workshop takeaway with specific method learned and how it changed your workflow.
- Post type 3: paper replication thread (what you reproduced, what matched, what did not).

Template:

```text
Question: [ecological question]
Data: [dataset/source]
Method: [model/workflow]
Result: [1 quantitative finding]
Artifact: [GitHub/poster/notebook link]
Next step: [what you are improving]
```
